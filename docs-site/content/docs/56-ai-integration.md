### AI collaboration, AI agents

- Access to files, not apps

- For example, tools like [Claude Code](chatgpt://generic-entity?number=0) (and similar) can work directly with your text files. In everyday use, this could mean asking an AI to reorganize an outline, summarize sections, maintain structured data, or generate new content—directly in the files you already use.

- I haven’t tested this extensively yet, but I plan to explore it and share my experiences.

- This is conceptually separate from TextToTile’s role. The app’s purpose is simply to present a text-based structure/outline in a clear visual way. Who—or what—maintains the text (on your local computer) is not important to the app.

- That said, TextToTile can be used to visualize dashboards, overviews, and plans that are partially or fully maintained by different AI agents, each handling specific tasks you define.
