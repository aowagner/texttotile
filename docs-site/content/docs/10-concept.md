## See Your Text in a New Way

TextToTile lets you explore structured text through visual overviews.

Write or edit ordinary text — in TextToTile or any text editor — and instantly see the same information visualized as structure, relationships, hierarchy, progress, or patterns.

The idea is intentionally simple, but surprisingly flexible. The same simple outline structure can be used for:

* a personal tracker
* a project plan
* a novel
* a travel overview
* a knowledge map
* a Kanban board
* a brainstorming space
* or something entirely your own

If information can be structured as text, TextToTile can visualize it.



## One Structure — Different Views

The same outline can be explored through different visual views. <kbd>Structure View</kbd> focuses on hierarchy and flow, while <kbd>Tag group View</kbd> highlights connections, themes, categories, and relationships across the same content.

As your outline grows, zoom and scaling allow you to view your text as a timeline and scroll and zoom smoothly between high-level overviews, detailed structure, individual entries, and larger patterns without changing the underlying text itself.

The visualization changes — but the information remains the same.



## A Tool for Thinking Visually

Rather than forcing information into a predefined system, TextToTile focuses on giving structure a visible form. This often makes it easier to spot gaps and patterns, understand relationships, track progress over time, organize complex information, and think more clearly at scale.

Because of this, TextToTile is not limited to one type of workflow. Some people may use it for writing and storytelling, while others may use it for planning, research, journaling, tracking, software projects, travel ideas, or simply organizing thoughts more clearly.



## Simple by Nature

At its core, TextToTile works with ordinary text files. There are:

* no accounts
* no mandatory cloud services
* no hidden databases
* no lock-in

Your files remain yours.

The app focuses on visualization and overview — while your actual information stays simple, readable, and fully under your own control.
