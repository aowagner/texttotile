TextToTile is a very simple app that does one thing: it visualizes a text outline in a graphical way.

Even though the app itself is simple, it opens up a wide range of possibilities for gaining visual overviews of many types of structured data and texts. At the same time, it helps simplify your documents and gives you full control over where your data is stored and who can access it.

For some types of data, this may not feel critical—for example, when writing a shopping list or drafting an article. But when working on something like a book, or tracking personal biometrics in text files, it can be reassuring to know that only those with access to your computer (typically just you) can view and modify the data. No one else can share your data.

### Many Benefits
This approach has many advantages. The app is generic—it is not designed for a specific type of data. An outline can represent the structure of anything you choose.

### Security
- Data security and privacy are top priorities in this app
- No data is sent to any server or backend
- No user information or activity is tracked
- Full anonymity—no accounts, no registration
